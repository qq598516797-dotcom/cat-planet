# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Cat lovers, portfolio reviewers, and casual visitors exploring a visual cat atlas on desktop first. They want to browse breeds, understand origin and personality, compare options, and save or share memorable results without needing technical knowledge.

## Product Purpose

Cat Planet is a 3D interactive cat atlas that turns breed information into an exploratory product experience. Success means visitors can quickly understand a cat, enjoy moving through the planet, use lightweight tools such as personality zones and comparison, and want to keep interacting beyond a single detail card.

## Brand Personality

Warm, polished, playful. The interface should feel healing and lively, but still controlled enough to look like a finished public GitHub project.

## Anti-references

Avoid field-dump encyclopedia pages, unclear feature names, decorative controls with no purpose, cluttered sticker or stamp grids, and heavy game systems with coins, levels, timers, or penalties.

## Design Principles

- PC first until the core experience is stable.
- Every feature must explain its purpose through the action it enables.
- Use real cat photos as the emotional anchor.
- Motion should clarify state changes and make interactions feel smooth.
- Keep playful systems lightweight, useful, and easy to exit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced-motion preferences, keep controls keyboard-accessible, preserve readable contrast, and avoid medical or purchase advice. Chinese and English copy should remain separated by language mode.
